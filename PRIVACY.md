# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 14, 2026  
**Last Updated:** March 14, 2026

At Vouchy Labs ("Vouchy," "we," "us," or "our"), we build Precision Social Proof Architecture to help brands showcase authentic human trust. We are committed to protecting the privacy of everyone who interacts with our services — whether you're a business collecting testimonials, a customer submitting a vouch, or a visitor viewing a Trust Page or widget.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and your choices. It applies to **vouchy.click**, the dashboard, Collectors (submission pages), Trust Pages (/trust/:slug), embedded widgets, and related features.

## 1. Information We Collect
- **From business users (account holders):** name, email, company name, payment information (handled securely by our payment processor), and usage data.
- **From testimonial givers:** name, profile image, submitted text, video (including audio), and answers to guided questions. Submitting is voluntary and does not require an account.
- **From all visitors:** IP address, browser/device type, pages viewed, timestamps, and performance metrics to keep everything fast and reliable.
- **Cookies & similar technologies:** essential cookies for core functionality (sessions, dark mode) and analytics cookies to understand how public pages perform. You can manage preferences through our cookie controls where available.

We do not collect sensitive personal information unless you voluntarily include it in a testimonial.

## 2. How We Use Information
- **To deliver the service:** host testimonials, power in-browser recording and teleprompter, display Trust Pages and widgets, and provide dashboard access.
- **To process and improve submissions:** transcribe/edit video/audio for quality, apply AI enhancements (text polishing, scripting suggestions).
- **For analytics and optimization:** understand engagement and refine features (anonymized where possible).
- **For communication:** send submission confirmations or account updates (you can opt out of non-essential messages).
- **To protect the platform:** prevent fraud, enforce our terms, and comply with legal obligations.

## 3. AI Features
- Our AI tools (AI Magic for text enhancement and AI-Assisted Scripting) use third-party providers (OpenAI and/or Anthropic).
- We do not use your personal testimonials to train third-party models. Providers are instructed not to retain or reuse your content beyond delivering the service.
- AI outputs may sometimes be inaccurate — always review before publishing.

## 4. Sharing
We do not sell personal information. We share only what is necessary with trusted service providers under strict agreements:
- **Database & authentication** → Supabase  
- **Video storage & fast delivery** → Cloudflare R2  
- **AI text processing** → OpenAI / Anthropic  
- **Analytics & performance** → Vercel

We may share anonymized or aggregated data for general insights. In the event of a merger, acquisition, or legal requirement, data may transfer with appropriate protections.

## 5. Retention & Security
- **Account data:** kept while your account is active and for a reasonable period afterward for legal or backup purposes.  
- **Testimonials:** kept until you delete them or your account ends (you control visibility and publication).  
- **Videos:** stored securely; unused drafts may be automatically removed after a set period.

We use encryption, access controls, and industry-standard practices to protect data, though no system is completely risk-free.

## 6. Your Choices & Rights
You can:
- Access, correct, export, or delete your data through the dashboard (business users) or by contacting us (testimonial givers / visitors).  
- Manage cookie preferences where shown.  
- Opt out of non-essential communications.

To exercise rights or ask questions: email **vouchytrust@gmail.com**. We aim to respond promptly.

## 7. Children's Privacy
Our services are not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children without appropriate consent.

## 8. Changes
We may update this policy. Material changes will be posted here with notice where appropriate.
